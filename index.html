About

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Chris Germano. I've been working with computers for pretty much my whole life. Starting at a young age, I taught myself animation with Flash, and quickly moved to ActionScript and Java programming. Over the years, I've designed and developed award-winning video games, built core components for Fortune 50 websites, led the development team of a New York startup, and created everything from Alexa Apps to Snapchat Filters.

These days, I work full-time at an IT Consultant and occasionally design games in my free time. I also play around with trading stocks, 3D printing, and manage a travel blog.
